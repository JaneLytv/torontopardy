How to play Torontopardy

  1. If you want to be lame and follow the rules, here's what you do: Read the card. Think of an answer. Click on the card to reveal the answer. Click the check mark if you got it right or the x if you got it wrong.
  2. Torontopardy is an honour system. If you want to make yourself feel better and bump up your numbers by clicking the checkmark until your finger falls off, who am I to stop you? Go ahead, lie to yourself. I won't judge you out loud.
  3. Tell your friends. A lot of hard work and hearty laughs went into making this game. Share it. Or don't. It's cool. Not like I'll be crying myself to sleep over it.
